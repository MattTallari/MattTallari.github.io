Project Description

Thhis was my first project in Unity and first time attempting to create a game from scratch. I chose to make a simple 2D platformer, but rather than make it scroll left to right I chose to make it scroll up and down to match the theme of climbing a tower.

The goal of the game is to collect treasure and coins while avoiding enemies and obstacles. Some enemies, like the turret, have a ranged attack that has a sensor that can tell whether the player is in range to shoot or not. Falling platforms and bounce pads also play a role in the platforming.

Tools/Skills Used

  • Unity v4
  • Code: C#
