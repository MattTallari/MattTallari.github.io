Project Description

This is my first experiment using 3DS Max's Matchmove feature and was part of an assignment in my 3D Graphics course. We were to film a short video anywhere we would have liked and then used the footage to import basic 3D objects to move in sync with the video. Through this assignment I learned the importance of matchmoving and what make s a good scene to matchmove, that being one with many reference points to make the objects in the video move more realisticly with the background.
