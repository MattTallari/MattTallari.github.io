Project Description

This photo was taken during the same time as my Gridlocked Video Project as both this photo and a shot in the video's location are the same. We were each assigned to select a landmark or building in Thunder Bay to take a photo of that would be considered for use in advertising some of Thunder Bay's sights and tourist locations. I chose the Italian Hall, and when taking the photo noticed it was fairly dull in the Winter with all the snow around and so I edited it to bring out the colour of the building.
